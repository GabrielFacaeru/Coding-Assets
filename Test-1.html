“Bober Kurwa,” also known as “Kurwa Bóbr” or “Bobrze,” is a meme centered around videos of Polish men recording beavers, often swearing or exclaiming in excitement upon spotting one. The trend began as early as 2012 on YouTube, with one of the earliest known videos being posted by YouTuber Krowi on October 5, 2012. The phrase “Bober” gained significant popularity as a meme on iFunny in 2018, and the trend continued to grow in popularity, spreading to TikTok by 2023. The term “Kurwa Bóbr” translates to “Damn Beaver” in English, where “kurwa” is a vulgar exclamation and “bóbr” means “beaver” in Polish. Videos featuring Polish people chasing and recording beavers have been widely shared and have garnered millions of views across various platforms.
